Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your hosting package?

Our enterprise hosting package includes SSL certificates, daily backups, 24/7 monitoring, CDN integration, DDoS protection, and unlimited bandwidth. We maintain 99.9% uptime and provide round-the-clock support.

How long does it take to complete a website project?

Most projects are completed within 2-4 weeks, depending on complexity and client feedback cycles. Simple landing pages can be done in 1 week, while complex e-commerce sites might take 6-8 weeks.

What ongoing support do you provide?

We offer comprehensive maintenance packages including security updates, content updates, performance optimization, and technical support. Our team is available via phone, email, and chat during business hours.

Do you offer custom functionality development?

Yes, we specialize in custom web development including e-commerce solutions, booking systems, customer portals, and API integrations. All custom features are thoroughly tested and documented.

What are your payment terms?

We offer flexible payment plans with a 50% deposit to start and the remainder due upon completion. Monthly payment plans are available for larger projects. We accept all major credit cards and ACH transfers.
